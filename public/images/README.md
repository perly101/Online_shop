## Required Images:

1. **logo of abs.jpg** - The logo is a gray outline of a building with multiple windows, representing the establishment Absolute Essential Trading.

   -The business name and tagline (“Absolute Essential Trading for Online Ordering and Pick-up System”) on the right or beside it.

## Image Guidelines:

- Use high-quality images for best results
- Ensure images are optimized for web (compressed but good quality)
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
- The CSS will automatically resize and crop images to fit the design
